Club Run

Start off the fall semester strong by running with Phi Kappa Alpha and learn what it means to be a part of the greatest club on campus. You will not regret choosing toRun Red !

Mud Fight

Join us for a night of food, fellowship, and fighting! Mud Fight is all about letting loose, meeting new people, and having good old fashioned fun.

Demo Derby

We dress down and embrace our southern roots at our annual Demo Derby filled with chili, cheering, and total mayhem!

Mrs. Gover's

One of our favorite traditions in Phi Kappa is visiting Mrs. Gover’s every Thursday for cookies, a devotional, and a hug from Mrs. Gover!

Guys Retreat

A retreat designed by our male officers to deepen your connection with God and strengthen your bonds with fellow brothers in Christ.

Girls Retreat

A retreat designed by our female officers where faith and friendship come together in a nurturing and uplifting environment.

Charge & Recharge

In Phi Kappa, we do not retreat… we Charge! Charge and Recharge are our retreats for both the guys and girls of Phi Kappa where we spend time growing closer to each other and closer to God.

Mardi Gras

We celebrate Mardis Gras right at our yearly crawfish boil! Join us for a lively event full of food and fellowship.

Riverboat

Our final event of the year is a formal night spent aboard a Memphis Riverboat. Riverboat is all about gratitude for the past year and hope for the year to come!
