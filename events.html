Club Run

Start off the fall semester strong by running with Phi Kappa Alpha. Come run with us to learn what it means to be apart of the greatest club on campus. We know you won't regret choosing to Run Red !

Mudfight

Dive into the messiest and most exciting event of the year—Mudfight! Join us for a day of pure fun as you and your friends revel in the ultimate muddy experience. This event is all about letting loose, enjoying great company, and embracing the chaos of a good, old-fashioned mudfight

Demo Durby

Rev up your engines and get ready for an adrenaline-pumping experience at Demo Derby! Join us for an action-packed event where the thrill of demolition meets the excitement of competitive driving. Watch as skilled drivers go head-to-head in a high-octane battle of endurance and skill, smashing and crashing their way to victory.

Mrs. Govers

A longstanding Phi Kappa tradition is to visit Mrs. Gover every Thursday during the semester. Come enjoy good company and great cookies after class!

Guys Retreat

Join us for a transformative Guys Retreat designed to deepen your connection with God and strengthen bonds with fellow men of faith. Set in a serene and inspiring environment, this retreat offers a mix of reflective activities, spiritual discussions, and personal growth opportunities. Engage in meaningful worship, prayer sessions, and faith-building workshops that will help you grow closer to God and to each other.

Girls Retreat

Discover a transformative experience at our Girls Retreat, where faith and friendship come together in a nurturing and inspiring environment. This retreat is designed to deepen your spiritual journey and strengthen connections with other women of faith. Participate in uplifting worship sessions, engaging Bible studies, and reflective prayer times that will enrich your relationship with God.

Charge & Recharge

Unplug and rejuvenate at the Charge/Recharge Retreat, a unique experience designed for both men and women seeking spiritual renewal and personal growth. Set in a tranquil setting, this retreat offers a chance to recharge your spirit through a series of dynamic sessions and reflective activities. Engage in inspiring worship, thought-provoking discussions, and moments of personal reflection that will help you deepen your connection with God.

Mardi Gras

Celebrate Mardi Gras with a taste of Southern tradition at our Crawfish Boil! Join us for a lively and flavorful event featuring heaps of fresh, perfectly seasoned crawfish, along with a variety of traditional sides and accompaniments. Whether you're a seasoned crawfish eater or new to this delicious tradition, our Crawfish Boil offers a chance to indulge in great food, good company, and an authentic Mardi Gras experience. Don your best Mardi Gras attire and come ready to feast, dance, and enjoy the festivities!

Riverboat

Join us for an unforgettable evening aboard the Memphis Riverboat, where elegance meets tradition. Our annual formal event promises a night of sophistication and celebration as we honor outstanding achievements within our club. Dress to impress and be prepared for a memorable experience on the water, surrounded by the stunning views of Memphis. Don’t miss this chance to celebrate with your fellow club members in style!
